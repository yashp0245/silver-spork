Girl Child Protection

Government Schemes

1. Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao (BBBP)

Launched in 2015, Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao is a nationwide campaign to address gender-biased sex selection and improve the status of girls in India. The program focuses on ensuring the survival and education of the girl child, with an emphasis on providing quality education and equal opportunities.

2. Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA)

Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan is a flagship program that aims to provide elementary education for all children, with a special focus on girls and children from marginalized communities. SSA supports infrastructure development, teacher recruitment, and various interventions to increase girls' enrollment and retention in schools.

3. Pragati Scholarship Scheme

The Pragati Scholarship Scheme, initiated by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), offers financial assistance to meritorious girl students pursuing technical and professional courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. It aims to empower women by enabling them to pursue higher education in technical fields.

4. National Scheme of Incentive to Girls for Secondary Education (NSIGSE)

NSIGSE provides financial incentives to encourage the enrollment and retention of girls in secondary education (classes 9 and 10). Girls who complete secondary education receive a one-time cash incentive, helping to reduce the gender gap in education at the secondary level.

5. Dhan Laxmi Scheme

The Dhan Laxmi Scheme is a conditional cash transfer program launched in several Indian states. It provides financial incentives to families for the birth of a girl child, her enrollment in school, and her completion of certain educational milestones. The scheme promotes both the birth and education of girls.
