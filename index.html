About us


  • Our mission is to empower women with tools to work their way out of poverty, care for their families, and strengthen their communities.
  • We envision a world without poverty in which women are empowered, uplifted, and equal partners in society.
  • We fund economic empowerment programs, including microfinance programs, small business development support, training, education, and other holistic poverty alleviation strategies, benefitting thousands of women experiencing poverty around the world.
  • Women can be leaders, doctors, teachers, or any profession they choose. They have the right to get a good education just like boys.
  • Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower women from all walks of life to realize their full potential and take control of their destinies. We envision a world where every woman has the confidence, skills, and resources to lead, succeed, and effect positive change in her life and community.
  • We advocate for gender equality in all aspects of life, from education and employment to decision-making and leadership roles.
  • We believe in the transformative power of education and strive to provide women with the knowledge and skills they need to thrive.
  • We provide scholarships, workshops, and mentorship programs to help women access quality education and develop essential skills.
  • We raise awareness about gender-based discrimination, violence against women, and the importance of women's rights through advocacy campaigns, seminars, and partnerships.
  • Over the years, we have witnessed countless success stories of women who have broken barriers, overcome challenges, and achieved their dreams with our support. These empowered women have, in turn, inspired others and become agents of change in their communities.
  • ur leadership programs equip women with the skills and confidence to take on leadership roles in their communities and beyond.
